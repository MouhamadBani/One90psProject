What Is One90ps?

Transforming Two of the World's Most Vital Sectors. One90ps is an ERP-style web platform currently under development, purpose-built for Healthcare and Agriculture organizations.

Whether you're running a hospital, managing a rural farm cooperative, or deploying regional healthcare programs, One90ps adapts to your operations and empowers smarter decision-making through intelligent automation and real-time insights.

Core Platform

Integrated Module System

Comprehensive tools designed specifically for healthcare and agriculture sectors, with shared infrastructure for seamless operations.

Healthcare Management

Complete patient management system with appointments, medical inventory, staff scheduling, billing & insurance integration, and comprehensive medical records management.

Agriculture Operations

Advanced farm & field management with crop cycle tracking, agri-finance tools, inventory management, soil & irrigation data, and worker coordination systems.

Shared ERP Core

Unified HR management, payroll processing, financial accounting, transaction tracking, and comprehensive budget planning across all operations.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Intelligent inventory movements, delivery optimization, vehicle management, route planning, and warehouse coordination systems.

Development Progress

Building the Future

Our systematic approach to creating a world-class ERP platform for healthcare and agriculture.

ERP-grade PostgreSQL Schema

Designed and implemented a robust, scalable database architecture that supports complex healthcare and agriculture operations with full data integrity.

Healthcare & Agriculture Core Models

Created comprehensive data models for patients, medical records, crop cycles, farm management, and all supporting business operations.

Mock Data Generation

Building realistic test data using Python and Faker to simulate real-world scenarios and ensure our platform handles complex operational data.

Backend API Development

Implementing RESTful APIs using Flask/FastAPI to power all platform operations with secure, efficient data access and manipulation.

Frontend UI & Dashboards

AI Insights & Advanced Features
